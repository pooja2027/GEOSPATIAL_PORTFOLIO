Key FindingsAnalysis
⚠️
Highest Risk: Chennai (88) and Nagapattinam (82) — high population density combined with active coastal erosion.
📏
Coastline ≠ Risk: Ramanathapuram has 486 km of coastline but only risk score 61, due to lower exposed population and higher wetland cover.
🌊
2015 — Systemic Event: Flood counts peaked in every district simultaneously, consistent with the extreme Northeast monsoon anomaly.
🏙️
Urbanisation Amplifies: Chennai (72% urban) records highest flood counts. Urban surfaces reduce absorption and increase runoff intensity.
🌿
Southern Districts Safer: Tirunelveli (44) and Thanjavur (48) have lower erosion rates, smaller exposed populations, and more forest cover.
